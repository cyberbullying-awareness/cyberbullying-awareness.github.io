Introduction

Cyberbullying attackers can have many reasons to why they bully but sometimes they don’t intend to bully.


REASONS OF CYBERBULLYING

Cyberbullies can bully others online because they have an issue at school, work, home or in a relationship, causing them to become frustrated. Then may then take out this frustration on others online because they may not know how to properly express their concerns and emotions. They may also do it for fun or entertainment and enjoy tormenting others or may see it as a way to stay popular or feel powerful or escape their current situation. Sometimes people Cyberbully because it helps them cope with their own low-self esteem or fit in with their peers, sometimes they do not want to do the bullying but are peer pressured or forced to. It can also be an error, the message could be misinterpreted or sent to the wrong individual.
