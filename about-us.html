ABOUT US

Who we are...

We are a group based in Trinidad and Tobago, providing information to the general public on Cyberbullying which is a criminal offense in many parts of the world, it can be a deadly crime. This website is created to provide detailed information to the public and also can be used for research by students. We would like to make a difference through this website by helping victims to understand what they are experiencing and ways to prevent and stop Cyberbullies and get help through the links for the helplines provided.
