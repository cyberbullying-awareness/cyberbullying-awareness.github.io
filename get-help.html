WAYS TO GET HELP

If you feel like you are a victim of Cyberbullying and it is affecting you in a negative way, causing suicidal thoughts or similar, you can call a helpline for the country you reside in. Also, reporting the incident to your nearest police station’s Cyberbullying/Cybercrime Unit help with the perpetrator.
To prevent further incidents with the attacker, ensure you have done the following:

1. Block the Cyberbully. Most social media platforms have ways of blocking users. This can come in the form as a “Block” button or “Ignore” button or similar.

2. Report the account of the Cyberbully. Most social media platforms have a report function, where users can report other users for breaking the rules of the platform.

3. Keep evidence as proof that the incident took place.

4. Get assistance from a friend or family member, adult or someone in authority.
